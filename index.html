titulo

On September 8, 1664, the English conquered the city of New Amsterdam and renamed it New York, in honor of the Duke of York and Albany. At the end of the Second Anglo-Dutch War, the Dutch gained control of Suriname in exchange for the English controlling New Amsterdam. New York City gained importance as a commercial port during the period of the British Empire. In 1754, the city's first higher education institution was founded, at Columbia University. During the American War of Independence, the city emerged as the scene of important battles. In 1789, the first president of the United States, George Washington, was announced at Federal Hall and New York remained the capital of the United States until the following year.
